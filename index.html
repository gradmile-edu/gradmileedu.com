Where to Study

Top Destinations for Higher Education

Explore countries offering world-class education and career opportunities

Europe

🇮🇹

Italy

🇮🇹 Italy

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Sciences, Architecture & Design
  • Tuition: €900 – €4,000/year (public)
  • Living Cost: €700 – €1,100/month
  • Language: English & Italian programs
  • Post-Study Work: 12-month residence extension
🇩🇪

Germany

🇩🇪 Germany

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Technology & Sciences
  • Tuition: €0 – €1,500/semester (public)
  • Living Cost: €850 – €1,200/month
  • Language: English & German programs
  • Post-Study Work: 18-month job seeker visa
🇫🇷

France

🇫🇷 France

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Sciences, Arts & Business
  • Tuition: €2,770 – €16,000/year
  • Living Cost: €800 – €1,200/month
  • Language: English & French programs
  • Post-Study Work: 2-year residence permit
🇳🇱

Netherlands

🇳🇱 Netherlands

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Business & Social Sciences
  • Tuition: €8,000 – €20,000/year
  • Living Cost: €900 – €1,300/month
  • Language: English (widely available)
  • Post-Study Work: 1-year Orientation visa
🇮🇪

Ireland

🇮🇪 Ireland

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Technology & Pharma
  • Tuition: €10,000 – €25,000/year
  • Living Cost: €900 – €1,500/month
  • Language: English
  • Post-Study Work: 2-year stay-back visa
🇸🇪

Sweden

🇸🇪 Sweden

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Innovation & Sustainability
  • Tuition: SEK 80,000 – 295,000/year
  • Living Cost: ~€800/month
  • Language: English (most Masters)
  • Post-Study Work: 12-month job seeker extension
🇪🇸

Spain

🇪🇸 Spain

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Business & Tourism
  • Tuition: €2,000 – €18,000/year
  • Living Cost: €700 – €1,000/month
  • Language: English & Spanish programs
  • Post-Study Work: 12-month job search permit
🇩🇰

Denmark

🇩🇰 Denmark

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Green Energy & Design
  • Tuition: €6,000 – €16,000/year
  • Living Cost: ~€900/month
  • Language: English (most Masters)
  • Post-Study Work: 6-month job search extension
🇵🇱

Poland

🇵🇱 Poland

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Medicine & Sciences
  • Tuition: €2,000 – €6,000/year
  • Living Cost: €400 – €700/month
  • Language: English programs available
  • Post-Study Work: 9-month job search permit

United Kingdom

🇬🇧

United Kingdom

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

  • Top Domain: Engineering, Business, Finance & STEM
  • Tuition: £12,000 – £38,000/year
  • Living Cost: £1,000 – £1,500/month
  • Language: English
  • Post-Study Work: 2-year Graduate Route visa

Canada
